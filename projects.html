Multiplication Game

This is a game built to test your multiplication skills! Once the game begins, you will have 40 seconds to answer as many questions right as you can. There are a total of three rounds and each round decreases by 10 seconds. Each correct answer will earn you one point while each incorrect answer will decrement your score by one. Once the timer hits 0 at the end of level 3, you will have hit the "game over" state.

HTML | CSS | JavaScript | DOM Manipulation

Give It A Shot

We all know the feeling: standing in a room full of people, unsure if you belong or what to say. In our case, everyone is ordering fancy cocktails and all you know how to say is "Vodka Soda". Give It A Shot changes all of that. As fledgling cocktail connoisseurs, we love trying new drinks. But, just like you, we don't even know what we don't know! Through Give It A Shot, we'll be able to try any drink known to the publicly available, Cocktail DB.

Once logged in, you will be taken to our drink quiz, starting with the type of liquor. You will also be able to specify an ingredient/flavor you like. From there, Give It A Shot will recommend you 2 brand new drinks! If you don't like those ones, you can always load more.

React | React Hooks | Retch API | Node.js | Express.js | Sequelize | PostgreSQL | Passport.js | bcrypt | Reactstrap

Fit-Init-2.0

I was inspired to create this app to help fitness instructors create workout plans for their clients using the wger Workout Manager API. As a fitness instructor myself, I wanted to build a tool that I could use with my own clients especially during the era of covid when in person training is not always an option.

Once logged in, you will be able to create a new workout with a custom workout type, workout name, and exercises. After creating a new entry, the workout with my available to view on the "My Workouts" page.
